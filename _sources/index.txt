Requirements for this workshop
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

- QGIS (version used for this workshop 2.18)
- `GeoPackage Extension for QGIS installed <https://github.com/pka/qgpkg>`_
- DB Browser for SQLite (version used for this workshop 3.10.1)
